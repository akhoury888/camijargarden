Our story

Made with calm,
made with precision,
made with love.

Cami's Jar Garden started the way most good things do — quietly, in a kitchen, on a slow Friday evening. Cami has always found peace in routines: the measuring, the layering, the waiting. There is something deeply satisfying about making one thing perfectly, every single week.

Each jar is a small, consistent act of care. The same attention goes into every layer — the oats, the fruit, the final touch. Cami prepares a limited number of jars per week, and no more. Because the best things are never rushed.

Plants are part of who Cami is. They find their way into the food — in herbs, in seeds, in the quiet green palette that surrounds it all. This isn't just overnight oats. It's a small garden for your morning.
